down and out in paris and london

author george orwell

I think "Down and Out in Paris and London" serves as a timeless reminder of the systemic issues within capitalist societies, shedding light on the enduring challenges of poverty, inequality, and the erosion of workers' rights. The parallels between Orwell's era and our own underscore the ongoing need for critical reflection and meaningful reform within our economic systems to address these deeply ingrained issues.
(tldr; late stage capitalism is a bitch)
